Shrum Biology

The Shrum Biology building lives up to its namesake. As the connector area for the ASB to the rest of SFU, it hosts a myriad of life including Science Alive. This volunteer program targets elementary to secondary age children in order to cultivate a love and appreciation for all matters Science. If you have time, you should check it out!
