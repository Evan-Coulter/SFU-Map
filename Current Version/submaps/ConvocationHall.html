Convocation Mall

Right downstairs from the AQ, Convocation Mall is an amazing social space for students and staff. This space leads to SFU's W.A.C. Bennett Library and its centre for student services, the Magie Benston Centre. Be sure to check out the DIY S'mores station open on most weekdays.
