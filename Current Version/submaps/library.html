W.A.C. Bennett Library

The Library of SFU Burnaby is a seven-story structure with area tailored to the different needs of each student. On the bottom floor, there's a large social space for students to study with friends while upper floors are more quiet, individual study spaces. It boasts a large collection of academic works that are available to any student that needs them. Workshops and other homework help is also an available resource at this location.
