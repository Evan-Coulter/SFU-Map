Blusson Hall

Gateway to UniverCity and Saywell Hall, Blusson hall offers large lecture halls and quiet study spot. As the closest entrance to SFU from the upper bus loop, it is often filled with students either heading to class or home. As one of the first spots that students are introduced to when arriving at SFU it remains a beautiful addition to campus with spacious classrooms along hallways with that are full of natural light.
